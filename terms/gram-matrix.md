[Wolfram Mathworld defines Gram matrix as][1]:

> Given a set $V$ of $m$ vectors (points in $\mathcal R^n$), the Gram matrix $G$ is
> the matrix of all possible inner products of $V$, i.e.,
> $$ g_{ij} = \mathbf v_i^T \mathbf v_j $$
